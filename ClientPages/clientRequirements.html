CHSD
City Housing and Settlements Department

Official document issued by the City Housing and Settlements Department, allowing an individual to legally construct, modify, or demolish a structure within their jurisdiction. It ensures that all construction work meets safety, health, and environmental protection standards.

Requirements:
  • HSD Form No.1
  • Building plan 5 sets
  • Proof of Ownership(TCT's Deed of sale or may applicant documents)
  • Photocopy of tax declaration & current tax receipt
  • HOA Clearance
  • Brgy. Clearance for Building Permit
  • Bill of materials and Specification
  • Photocopy of PRC IC/PTR of all signing engineers
  • Certificate of Exemption Location Clearance from CPDO
  • Structural Analysis(2-Storey and up)
  • Fire Clearance
  • Boring Test(3-Storey Building)
  • Occupancy Permit(Home Improvement/extension)

Requirements:
  • Duly Accomplished Certificate of Building Completion Form, SIgned and Sealed by Architect/Civil Engineer, Professional Electrical Engineer, Sanitary Engineer.
  • Photocopy of Approved Building
  • Permit Form
  • Photocopy of Approved Plan
  • Photocopy of Structure: Front, Back, Left, Right, Lavatory, and CT.
  • Sketch of Location and Contact Number

Requirements:
  • Photocopy of Meralco Service Application
  • Photocopy of Barangay Clearance
  • Proof of Ownership:
    1. Photocopy of Land Title
    2. Photocopy of Deed of Sale + Xerox copy of title(original owner)
    3. Photocopy of Lease Purchase Agreement + Xerox Copy of title HOA
    4. HSD Certification/Certifies True Copy of Award
    5. Notarized Undertaking if the Title is not named after the Applicant
  • Photocopy of Certificate of Occupancy-Building Permit
  • Picture of Structure and Contact Number

Who may avail: All residents of the City of Calamba aged 18-55 years old without owning their own land.

Note: Requirements marked with "*" are situational and depend on your current circumstances.
